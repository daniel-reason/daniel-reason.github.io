Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

The Fogg Method of creating tiny habits I found very simplistic and easy to try and with some success too. I already incorporate my own kind of meditation through sport being able to clear my mind and create a single focus. Often refered to by sportsmen as "The Zone".

What did you find interesting?

The Pomodoro Technique is very interesting and something that I will try to adopt. I will often get either distracted easily or be able to focus on a single task for a long period of time which ultimately burns me out. I am hoping this will create a good balance for me.

What worked for you?

The Fogg Method of creating tiny habits. My room has never been cleaner haha.

Which will you experiment further with?

Both the Fogg Method and the Pomodoro Technique. I will obviously carry on with my sports meditation but may try the standard meditation style also.
